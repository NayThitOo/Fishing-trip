About this site

This simple site highlights fishing locations, culture and safety tips for visitors and locals in Myanmar. It is a demo HTML site with three pages — use it as a starter and replace media with your own files.

Why fish in Myanmar?

Myanmar's rivers and lakes offer a mix of scenic beauty and abundant fish species. Local fishing supports many families and communities; when visiting, choose responsible operators.
